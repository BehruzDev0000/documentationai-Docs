## Add Your Project

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to the dashboard">
    ````
    Visit https://portal.behruztoshpulatov.com after login.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create project">
    ````
    Click the "Create New Project" button and enter project details.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start documenting">
    ````
    Use "Add Page" to create new documentation pages for your project.
    Organize them with folders for better structure.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
