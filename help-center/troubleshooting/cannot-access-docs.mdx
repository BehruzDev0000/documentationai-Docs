---
title: Cannot Access Documentation Pages
description: Quick fixes when you can't view your docs.
---

## Symptoms

- Pages don't load after login
- 404 or "Page Not Found" errors

## Possible Causes

- Not logged in
- Project permissions not set

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check login status">
    ````
    Make sure you are logged into the portal.
    If not, visit https://portal.behruztoshpulatov.com/login
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions">
    ````
    Ensure you have access to the selected project in your Workspace settings.
    Contact the project owner if needed.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
