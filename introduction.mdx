## Overview

Welcome to Behruz Toshpulatov's documentation platform. You get a centralized space to organize, manage, and share all your project documentation effortlessly. Whether you work on software projects, research initiatives, or team wikis, this platform helps you create structured docs, collaborate seamlessly, and keep everything up to date.

You benefit from intuitive tools that scale with your needs. Teams gain version control and real-time edits, while individuals enjoy simple markdown editing and publishing. Start building your knowledge base today.

<Callout kind="info">
  This platform supports Markdown, MDX components, and custom themes with your brand color `{#3B82F6}`.
</Callout>

## Quick Start

Get up and running in minutes.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create Account" icon="user-plus">
    Sign up at `https://app.behruztoshpulatov.com/register` with your email.
  </Step>
  <Step title="New Space" icon="plus">
    Click "New Documentation Space" and name it for your project.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Pages" icon="file-plus">
    Create your first page using the editor. Try this sample:

````markdown
```javascript
console.log("Welcome to your docs!");
```
````

  </Step>
  <Step title="Publish" icon="globe">
    Hit publish to share your live site at `https://docs.behruztoshpulatov.com/your-space`.
  </Step>
</Steps>
